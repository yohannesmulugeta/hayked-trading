# Hayked Client Content Checklist

Use this checklist before changing the demo into a production website.

## Brand and company

- Official approved logo and brand guideline
- Correct legal company name
- Approved company introduction
- Verified mission and vision
- Founder or management biography
- Professional leadership portraits
- Business registration or licence information approved for public display

## Coffee offers

For each current coffee offer:

- Coffee name or lot reference
- Origin and source area
- Grade
- Process
- Crop year
- Variety where verified
- Altitude where verified
- Physical specification
- Cup profile or score where approved
- Available quantity
- Packaging
- Shipment period
- Specification PDF
- Approved coffee image

## Processing and warehousing

- Verified facility location
- Approved service list
- Processing stages
- Machinery information
- Processing capacity
- Warehouse capacity
- Lot-separation procedure
- Storage-control procedure
- Dispatch workflow
- Real exterior, interior and equipment photos

## Quality control

- Sampling procedure
- Physical inspection procedure
- Moisture and other tests performed
- Cupping or evaluation procedure
- Roles responsible for approval
- Sample approval workflow
- Available buyer documents
- Current certifications and their valid documents

## Sustainability and sourcing

- Sourcing model
- Supplier or producer relationships
- Traceability approach
- Workforce practices
- Community activities
- Environmental practices
- Current certifications, memberships or audit evidence

## Contact and commercial flow

- Main export email
- Sales contact name
- Primary and secondary telephone numbers
- WhatsApp number
- Office and facility addresses
- Working hours
- Expected enquiry response time
- Sample courier procedure
- Current offer-list process

## Media

- Hero photography
- Coffee cherries and farms
- Processing operation
- Warehouse interior and exterior
- Green coffee samples
- Quality-control and cupping
- Bagging and loading
- Team photographs
- Company profile PDF
- Service brochure PDFs
